About Me

Alperen is not your traditional growth marketer. With a Mathematics (Honors) & Mathematical Economics degree from the University of Rochester, he combines deep analytical rigor with hands-on expertise across the full spectrum of paid acquisition, lifecycle marketing, and product-led growth.
