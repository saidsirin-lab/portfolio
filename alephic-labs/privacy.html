Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 9, 2026

Overview

Alephic Labs ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to transparency about how we collect and use data. This policy explains the limited data we collect when you visit our website at alephiclabs.com.

Data We Collect

Analytics Data (Google Analytics 4)

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to collect anonymous, aggregated data about how visitors interact with our website. This helps us understand which pages are visited, how users navigate the site, and how we can improve the experience.

The data collected by GA4 includes:

  • Pages viewed and time spent on each page
  • Referring source (how you arrived at our site)
  • General geographic region (country/city level, not precise location)
  • Device type, browser, and operating system
  • Interaction events (clicks, scrolls, form submissions)

This data is collected using cookies set by Google Analytics. The data is processed in aggregate and does not personally identify you. We do not collect names, email addresses, or any personal information through analytics.

Form Submissions

When you voluntarily submit a form on our website (such as the agency application, Growlytics AI early access request, or contact form), we collect the information you provide (name, email, company, etc.). This data is used solely to respond to your inquiry and is sent to our business email at contact@alephiclabs.com.

How We Use Your Data

  • To understand website traffic and user behavior in aggregate
  • To improve our website content, layout, and performance
  • To respond to inquiries submitted through our forms
  • To measure the effectiveness of our marketing efforts

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies from Google Analytics to collect usage data. You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may limit the data we collect but will not affect your ability to browse the website.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics 4 — website usage analytics (Google Privacy Policy)
  • Vercel — website hosting (Vercel Privacy Policy)
  • Google Fonts — typography rendering

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on
  • Request deletion of any personal data you have submitted via our forms
  • Contact us with any questions about your data
